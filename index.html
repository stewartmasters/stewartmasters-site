AI in Practice

Most AI projects fail
in the integration layer —
not the model.

The hard part of AI isn't choosing a model. It's embedding it into workflows that real teams use under real pressure — with imperfect data, legacy systems, and people who weren't asking for it.

I've built AI into operations across multiple international markets. Not demos. Not pilots. Running systems that make decisions daily.

Where I've been involved externally, the focus has been on integration, adoption, and real outcomes.

What I focus on:

  • → Connecting AI to the decisions that matter
  • → Building for adoption, not just capability
  • → Measuring outcomes, not activity
