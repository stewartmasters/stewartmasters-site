AI Governance for Boards and NEDs

Boards are increasingly responsible for AI governance without the frameworks to do it effectively. This session is designed specifically for board members and NEDs — focused on oversight, risk, and accountability rather than technical detail.

What I cover

  • →What AI governance actually means at board level — and what it doesn't
  • →The specific AI risks that boards are responsible for overseeing
  • →How to evaluate whether management's AI strategy is credible
  • →The questions every board member should be asking — and red flags in the answers
  • →What good AI governance looks like in practice, with examples from different sectors

Available formats

Boardroom session (90 min) NED education session (half-day) Keynote (45 min) Panel discussion

Who this is for

  • ·Boards and non-executive directors
  • ·Chairs and governance committees
  • ·Investors and audit committees
  • ·Governance and risk conferences
