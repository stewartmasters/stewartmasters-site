Formats

Depending on the audience and objective, sessions can take different forms.

01

Keynote

30–60 minutes for a conference, summit, or company event. Designed to challenge assumptions and leave the audience with a clearer framework for thinking about AI and digital change.

02

Executive Workshop

Half or full-day sessions with leadership teams. Structured around your specific context and challenges, with time to work through real decisions rather than generic frameworks.

90-minute sessions designed for boards and NEDs. Focused on governance, oversight, and the right questions to ask — rather than technical detail.

Panel participation at conferences and industry events, particularly on AI strategy, digital leadership, and the intersection of technology and business governance.
